About Us




HISTORY

The founder of this company Johnny Joestar discover his enthusiasm towards mechanincal keyboards in the year of 2002. He started his keyboard journey through his first custom pc and fell in love immediately, and thought that mechanical keyboards deserve the love and community that's why he created this company.


MISSION & VISION

Our mission is to provide the most convenience services to our customer no matter the request. Our vision is to bring as much people to the community of mechanical keyboards, to let people realize the beauty of custom mechanical keyboards.

Our core values are QUALITY, TRUST & EFFICIENCY. We only give out the most quality keyboards to our customers, low quality keyboard is not acceptable in keebs. We are a man of our words, we do whatever we have told the customers, we built trust with our customers. We provide the most fastest service possible to our customer, not more than 2 weeks. Satisfying customers is the best we can do.
