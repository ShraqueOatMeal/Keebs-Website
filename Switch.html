Switch
SWITCHES THE HEART OF THE KEYBOARD

Switches are the core of a keyboard as they controls the whole feel of a keyboard and sound. For new comers in the keyboard community, basically switches varies in 3 category linear, tactile and clicky. A linear is basically no bumpy feel. a tactile with a bumpy feel and clicky with a bumpy feel and a click sound.
